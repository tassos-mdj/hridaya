It is an evolutionary and empowering method which helps students experience the movements of prane (life force) and explore their energetic bodies through intelligent yoga sequences and movement meditations.

BIO

My name is Nastazia Spyridonos and I am a 500hrs RYT certified Prana Vinyasa Yoga teacher. My contact with yoga began in 2017 without knowing that it would completely change my life. In 2019, I practiced Prana Vinyasa Flow Yoga for the first time, a system created and spread all over the world to this day by Shiva Rea. Having practiced Hatha and Iyengar yoga, it was only with Prana Vinyasa that I felt truly connected to my body and my emotions while my concentration, strength and flexibility got noticeably improved. So during the same year I decided to attend my first 200-hour teacher training, which I completed in 2020. Over the course of two years, I obtained two additional certifications in Elemental Prana Vinyasa. In June 2025, I completed my 300-hour advanced training under the guidance of Shiva Rea herself. Prana Vinyasa is a system that integrates breath with movement, encouraging deep connection with the body and its needs in the present moment. The practice unfolds as a conscious, harmonious flow that invites both awareness and transformation. For the past two and a half years, I have been teaching yoga professionally, offering both private and group classes, as well as on-demand Online Yoga Sessions through the international platform TEACHABLE. I enjoy sharing the beautiful, never-ending journey of Yoga, evolving along with my students and helping them connect with their bodies and discover the limitless potential which they might not have known they posess.
